Our manufacturing base

GTIT

Founded in 2008, Ningbo Geartorque Intelligent Transmission(GTIT) Co., Ltd. is a high-tech manufacturer dedicated to the R&D and production of precision planetary transmission components. Headquartered in Ningbo, China, we specialize in core reducer parts including planetary gears, internal ring gears and gear shafts. In addition to customized precision gear solutions, we also offer full gear processing technology transfer services.

“ Fast forward to today, and we’re proud to be a thriving team, working on projects of all shapes and sizes, still fueled by the same passion and curiosity that got us started. 

Max Meng

Founder, GTIT

With over eighteen years of expertise in the power transmission sector, we have built a complete precision manufacturing system equipped with advanced machining, heat treatment and testing facilities. We can handle the entire production process in-house, exercising rigorous control over gear profile accuracy, lead accuracy and overall product performance. All products are made from high-grade alloy steel and premium raw materials, featuring outstanding precision, wear resistance and operational stability. We serve global clients with both mass production of standard products and custom non-standard gear manufacturing.
By deploying MES intelligent manufacturing systems and PLM product lifecycle management systems, we keep enhancing production efficiency and quality management. Upholding the principles of precision, reliability and responsive service, Geartorque delivers professional planetary transmission solutions to customers across the globe.
