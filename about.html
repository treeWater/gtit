Our manufacturing base

GTIT

Founded in 2008, Ningbo Geartorque Intelligent Transmission (GTIT) Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise dedicated to the R&D and production of precision planetary transmission components and humanoid robots. Headquartered in Ningbo, China, we specialize in core reducer parts — including planetary gears, internal ring gears, and planet carriers — that power industries worldwide.

“ We have never chased scale for its own sake. Every gear we cut, every robot we build, is a commitment to doing it right — precisely, reliably, and on time. That is the promise we started with, and the one we intend to keep. 

Max Meng

Founder, GTIT

What We Deliver
Beyond customized precision gear solutions and manufacturing technology services, we provide complete turnkey factory setup services — from process design and equipment planning to production know-how and commissioning support. Whether you are launching a new facility or upgrading an existing line, we bring the expertise to get you into production, faster.

Humanoid Robotics
Through our dedicated subsidiary, we design and manufacture fully assembled humanoid robots and their key components — backed by comprehensive maintenance and after-sales service. From individual parts to complete robotic systems, we are your single-source partner.

Precision Without Compromise
With over eighteen years of expertise in the power transmission sector, we have built a complete precision manufacturing system equipped with advanced machining, heat treatment, and testing facilities. Every step — from raw material to finished product — is handled in-house under rigorous control over gear profile accuracy, tooth lead accuracy, and overall performance. All products are crafted from high-grade alloy steel and premium materials, delivering outstanding precision, wear resistance, and operational stability.

Built for Scale
We serve global clients with both mass production of standard products and custom non-standard gear solutions. Powered by MES intelligent manufacturing and PLM product lifecycle management systems, we continuously drive efficiency and quality forward. Upholding the principles of precision, reliability, and responsive service, Geartorque delivers professional planetary transmission solutions and intelligent robotic systems to customers across the globe.
